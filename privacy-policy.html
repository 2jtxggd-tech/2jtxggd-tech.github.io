Arrow Scroller

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Arrow Scroller is an iPad Safari extension app that helps users control their own scrolling while reading long pages in Safari.

Information Stored on Your Device

Arrow Scroller does not automatically send user information to the developer's servers or to third-party servers. The app does not use accounts, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

To provide its features, Arrow Scroller may store the following information on your device:

  • Safari extension settings
  • Settings such as panel position, theme, and scroll speed
  • Host names used for per-site speed settings
  • Local usage counters, such as scroll actions and assisted scrolling time
  • Optional support notes that you choose to enter in the app

This information is stored on your device and is not automatically sent to the developer.

Web Page Information

Arrow Scroller runs as a Safari extension on pages where you allow it. It shows a small scrolling control panel and performs scrolling on your device in response to your own actions.

The app does not automatically send your browsing URL, page title, page content, form input, search history, or browsing history to the developer.

Support Notes and Exports

Optional support notes are stored on your device. If you choose to export support data, the app can create files such as PDF, CSV, or JSON for your own review or support request.

Support data may include your support note, category, date, app version, device type, OS version, usage count, scroll operation count, assisted scrolling time, normal stop count, export date, selected period, an internal ID for each note, and counts or trends derived from support notes, categories, app versions, and device types. The internal note ID is not a user ID or device ID. It does not include browsing URLs, page titles, page content, or form input.

Nothing is sent automatically. The developer receives support data only if you manually share it with support, such as by attaching an exported file to an email. If you send a support email using the system Mail app, the developer receives that email's sender address. Arrow Scroller does not automatically access or send your email address. Shared support data is used only to respond to your support request, may be associated with the sender, and is not used for tracking.

Purpose of Use

Information stored on device is used to keep settings, provide the scrolling assistance features, manage in-app review prompts, and help you prepare optional support requests.

Arrow Scroller is not intended for unauthorized automation, bots, traffic generation, or external data collection.

Retention and Deletion

Settings and optional support notes remain on your device while you use the app. You can delete support notes in the app. If you delete the app, data stored by the app is normally removed from the device by the operating system.

Third-Party Sharing

Arrow Scroller does not sell user data or share it with third parties for advertising, marketing, tracking, or data broker purposes.
