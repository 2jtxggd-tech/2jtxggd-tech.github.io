When contacting support, please include your iPad model, iPadOS version, what happened in Safari, and the steps that reproduce the issue if you know them.

Support Notes and Exported Files

Arrow Scroller includes optional support notes that are stored on your device. Support data stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

An exported support file may include your support note, category, date, app version, device type, OS version, usage count, scroll operation count, assisted scrolling time, normal stop count, export date, selected period, an internal ID for each note, and counts or trends derived from support notes, categories, app versions, and device types. The internal note ID is not a user ID or device ID. It does not include browsing URLs, page titles, page content, or form input.

Nothing is sent automatically. The developer receives support data only if you manually share it with support, such as by attaching an exported file to an email. If you send a support email using the system Mail app, the developer receives that email's sender address. Arrow Scroller does not automatically access or send your email address. Shared data is used only for support, may be associated with the sender, and is not used for tracking.

App Purpose

Arrow Scroller helps users control their own scrolling while reading long pages in iPad Safari. It is not intended for unauthorized automation, bots, traffic generation, or external data collection.

Privacy

The app does not require an account and does not use ads, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs. Settings, usage counters, and optional support notes are stored on device.
