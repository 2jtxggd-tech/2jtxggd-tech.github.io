Support

For bugs, questions, or feature requests about Arrow Scroller, please contact us by email.

Contact

Support email:

arrowscroller.support@gmail.com

When contacting support, please include your iPad model, iPadOS version, what happened in Safari, and the steps that reproduce the issue if you know them.

In-App Support Email

In the app, choose a category and enter your request to create an editable email draft with your device model, OS, app version, and build number. The device model may include a non-unique hardware model identifier, but never a device-specific identifier.

You can review, edit, or delete any details before sending. The email contents are not saved in the app or sent automatically. Only if you send the draft from your email app does the developer receive the sender email address, support request content, and the listed device information for customer support only.

App Purpose

Arrow Scroller helps users control their own scrolling while reading long pages in iPad Safari. It is not intended for unauthorized automation, bots, traffic generation, or external data collection.

Privacy

The app does not require an account and does not use ads, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs. Settings and local usage counters are stored on device; support request text is not retained by the app.
